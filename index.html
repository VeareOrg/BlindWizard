In the world of Antreia there is a blind young man capable of doing incredible things. His name is Jasper and he is just discovering his special abilities. However, since he was a small child he could navigate and he was never getting lost, despite the fact that he couldn't see a thing. But his abilities don't end on that as you can find out in the game.


Audio Trailer:




The Blind Wizard of Antreia is a Sound-Novel, whitch means is a kind od interactive audio book. The game consists of sounds only, so it doesn't contain any graphics. You can use keyboard/gamepad to navigate. You hear spacial sounds so you know if something is to the left or in front of you. You can find challenging sound riddles in the game. The project is a WIP (work in progress).

Navigate

Follow the sounds, move around in the world.

3D sound effects

You can hear events, people and objects from different directions.

Riddles

You have to face different challanges along your way.

Accesibility

Created with blind people in mind.
